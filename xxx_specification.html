This website's goal is to give users a place to conveniently obtain details about restaurants in their neighborhood. Customers can get information on restaurant locations, phone numbers, hours of operation, menu prices, and reviews on the website..

People looking for restaurants in their area make up the bulk of this website's target audience. They will receive the information they need to decide which restaurant to visit through the website, which will be configured to their needs. The information offered on the website will be accurate and current, and the website will be simple to use.

The website will contain the following pages:

  • Home page
  • About
  • Services
  • Search page
  • Restaurant listings page
  • Restaurant details page
  • Contact page
  • Booking form
  • Feedback form
  • Register form

The website will provide customers with the following features:

  • Customers can use a search box to look for restaurant within a certain distance from where they are.
  • Restaurants in the designated area are listed, along with information about their locations, contact information, and feedback from previous customers.
  • Detailed view page with additional information such as menus, pricing and images.
  • Feedback form to allow customers to leave reviews and rate their experience.

There are several existing websites providing customers with information regarding restaurants in their area. These include:

  • TripAdvisor
  • Yelp
  • Zomato
